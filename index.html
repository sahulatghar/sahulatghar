Virtual Assistant

A virtual assistant (VA) is an independent contractrator who provide adminstrative services to client while operating outside of the client's office.

  1. Manage an Amazon Account
  2. Understand the use of different data research tools for Product Research and Product Sourcing for Amazon.
  3. Order Fulfillment tasks starting from Inventory replenishment to shipping FBM orders to customers.
  4. Providing customer service for Amazon Customers on client's behalf and handling Returns, Refunds.
  5. Management of an Amazon Listing from inception to optimization.
  6. Advertisement campaign Management for Amazon (PPC).
  7. Product lunch and Ranking
  8. Working on different plateform to search Client, Upwork and Fiverr as Amazon Virtual Assistant.

FBM

Fulfillment by Merchant (FBM) is when the seller is in control of his entire handling and shipping process. Instead of paying a service fee and shipping inventory to Amazon to handle, the seller uses his or her own resources and sends the items directly to the buyer.

Private Label (PL)

Private label brands (or own brand labels) are products sold by a retailer with its own packing, but manufactured by a third party. Pick any product which is easily available, brand it,pack it, Name it, registered it and ask to amazon to list it with your own name.
